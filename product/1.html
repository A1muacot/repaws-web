About Us

Hello from RePaws!

Born from a love for pets and nature, RePaws transforms pineapple peels - an overlooked agricultural by-product - into a sustainable pet care ecosystem. With enzyme-powered, 100% natural formulas, we deliver safe, effective, and eco-friendly solutions for pet hygiene. We believe that caring for pets also means caring for the planet. Every RePaws product is a step toward reducing agricultural waste, supporting local farmers, and protecting the well-being of both animals and their owners. Let's build a greener, healthier world - together, one paw at a time.

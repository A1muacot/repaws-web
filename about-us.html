About Us

Hello from RePaws!

Born from a love for pets and nature, RePaws transforms pineapple peels - an overlooked agricultural by-product - into a sustainable pet care ecosystem. With enzyme-powered, 100% natural formulas, we deliver safe, effective, and eco-friendly solutions for pet hygiene. We believe that caring for pets also means caring for the planet. Every RePaws product is a step toward reducing agricultural waste, supporting local farmers, and protecting the well-being of both animals and their owners. Let's build a greener, healthier world - together, one paw at a time.

Our journey

1

We arrived in Tiền Giang - a province over 1,700 km from Hanoi, Vietnam.

2

The sweet scent of pineapples fills the air, but something stands out - massive piles of discarded pineapple peels.

3

Curious, we asked a local farmer:
“What happens to all these pineapple peels?”

4

“We either burn them or throw them away - there's just too much,” the farmer sighed.

We realized a problem

Inspired by that, we aim to transform pineapple waste into ...

... a sustainable ecosystem of enzyme-powered pet care solutions.

Pineapple Enzyme-Based Pet Care Products

Our innovative RePaws line transforms pineapple waste into pet care products. Fermented peel extracts deliver natural enzymes and probiotics that clean effectively while supporting skin health. This sustainable solution captures the growing natural pet market while solving agricultural waste challenges.
